The Ecological Impact of Digital

As we launch our fifth year of the Digital Detox, DLINQ is venturing into a less familiar space for our digital learning experts—how digital learning and technologies impact the Earth and our relationship with it. This venturing comes from a place of deep concern—climate experts warn that (cite some data here)…

We wonder, how does our digital learning work exacerbate climate change and its impacts? How might we rethink what we do as a group, as part of an institution so focused on environmental action? How might digital technologies support the work of environmental action, and how do we think about their benefits and tradeoffs?

Through this detox, we’ll share what we learn in ways that invite others to reflect on the impact of their technology use on the environment.

We will turn often to climate experts to help us make sense of our wonderings and questions. We will also look to you, our readers, to share your expertise on these topics.
